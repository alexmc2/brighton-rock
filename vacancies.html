Our Application Process

Application Process at Brighton Rock

The application process is as follows:

When vacancies arise, we invite those interested in joining us to visit the co-op where they can view the properties, meet some of our members, and ask any questions they may have. Application forms will be provided at the viewing, which should be completed and returned by the specified date.

Your attendance at a general meeting would also be advantageous so that you can get a better understanding of how the co-op works. Our monthly meetings are typically on weekday evenings at 7:30PM and the dates can be found here

The shortlisting is based on the following criteria:

  • Housing need
  • Ability to pay rent (from wages or benefits) and expected duration of tenancy
  • Relevant skills and what you can bring to the co-op
  • Willingness to share, respect others, and uphold equal opportunities
  • Ability to actively engage and work within a group
  • Compatibility with co-op in terms of lifestyle and work

If you are invited for an interview, our allocations officer will inform you of the time and place and at least five day's notice will be given. The interview is scheduled on a specified weekend day, and all members of the co-op will be present. Unfortunately, due to the difficulty co-ordinating the weekend availability of members, no flexibility can be offered for the interview date.

During the interview, each member will in turn ask the applicant a question. The questions and format of the interview are identical for every applicant. You will also have the opportunity to ask us any questions that you may have at this stage. We understand a panel interview can be intimidating, so we aim to make it as relaxed as possible.

If successful at the first interview, applicants will be invited to a more casual meeting over a cup of tea with their potential housemates. This provides an opportunity for both current members and applicants to gauge if they would be a good fit for each other. Notification of your application's status will follow this meeting.

New members will connect with the secretary and rent officer to sign the tenancy agreement and membership forms. You are expected to move in and start paying rent within one month, unless other arrangements have been agreed.

If you want to be notified of future vacancies, please contact our allocations officer at: vacanciesbtnrock@gmail.com
